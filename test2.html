Our Story

Founded in 2020, Salad Sasacil began as a small farmers market stand with a simple mission: to make healthy eating delicious and accessible.

Today, we've grown into a beloved local salad destination, but we've stayed true to our roots. Every salad is still prepared fresh daily using the highest quality ingredients from trusted local suppliers.
